About Us


Welcome to Travello, your number one source for all things travel. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of travel, with an emphasis on experience, affordability and safety.

Founded in 1992 by Alex Ferguson, Travello has come a long way from its beginnings in Mumbai. When Ferguson first started out, his passion for the best travel experience drove him to start his own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Mission and Vision


Honesty

Alemana Tours works with honest employees, with honest, fair and competitive market prices. We strive to demonstrate honest behavior towards our environment (clients, suppliers and other parties who we conduct business with).

Transparency

Alemana Tours strive to be clear in our products, services and processes. Be transparent to our direct environment.

Quality

Clients come in the first place, Alemana Tours strive for an optimal service level in order to get the clients best value for his tour. To achieve this we look for the best people and operators involved in our business and we design our services and products in the best way.

Personal

Alemana Tours like to give tailor made advice as every person is different with different wishes. It means personal commitment, aimed on establishing a temporarily, personal bond which enables us to really help you to get the best value for your tour. It also means a friendly service with a smile, with some humor.

Sustainable tourism

Alemana Tours conducts business in a sustainable way. We also take into account the interest of the local people, preserve the cultural heritage, we take care of our environment and focusing economically on long term results and fair economic fair practices.

Professional

We take our business very serious. It means our behavior is correct, objective, balanced, efficient and reliable. We are down to earth in the way we conduct business.

Community

10% of Travello's total profits are sent to our charitable trust, which aims at providing children across the world with a quality education and upbringing, and helping families around the world maintain a fair standard of living.
